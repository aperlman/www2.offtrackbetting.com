AMERICAN PHAROAH IS RACING THIS SUNDAY IN THE HASKELL INVITATIONAL

The Grade 1 Haskell Invitational takes place at Monmouth Park on the Jersey shore this upcoming weekend. Featuring the nation's top 3-year-old Thoroughbreds, it will mark the first return to the track for Triple Crown Winner - American Pharoah! The Zayat Stables color-bearer became the first Triple Crown winner in 37 years with his victory in the Belmont Stakes having taken the Preakness and Kentucky Derby prior to that. Last year's 2-year-old Champion will enter the Haskell having won seven races in a row.

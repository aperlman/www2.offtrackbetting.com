2016 Preakness Stakes Preview, Entries & Odds

ADVANCED WAGERING is now available for he 141st running of the Preakness Stakes. The second jewel of the Triple Crown at Pimlico Race Course in Baltimore, Maryland, will feature an 11-horse field including Kentucky Derby winner Nyquist.

The J. Paul Reddam-owned horse will make look to remain undefeated in its ninth competitive race. Nyquist will post third out of the starting gate and opened as a 3-5 betting favorite on Saturday. His toughest competitor will come from the five post in Exaggerator, currently going off at 3-1. Exaggerator has finished second to Nyquist in two different races, never conquering the undefeated colt.

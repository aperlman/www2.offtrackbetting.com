Unbeaten McCraken favorite for Keeneland's Blue Grass Stakes

OPENING WEEKEND AT KEENELAND - McCraken, No. 1 in the latest NTRA top 3-year-old poll and the slight favorite in the Kentucky Derby Futures Pool, drew the No. 2 post in the $1 million Blue Grass on Tuesday and was named the morning-line favorite at odds of 7-5.

O'neill Enters Four & Baffert Has Three In Premier Showdown

Saturday is the biggest day of the year at Santa Anita - the 80th running of the $1 million Santa Anita Derby, with the winner moving on to the Kentucky Derby on the first Saturday in May. Top prospects Iliad, Gormley, Reach the World and American Anthem all figure prominently among a field of 13 sophomores.

93rd Running of Wood Memorial Promises At Least One Spot in the Kentucky Derby.

Run at 1 1/8 miles at Aqueduct, the $1 million Wood Memorial Stakes is worth 170 Kentucky Derby points, distributed to the first four finishers on a 100-40-20-10 basis. Wood Memorial alumni have won the Kentucky Derby 20 times. The last horse to exit the Wood and win the Kentucky Derby was Funny Cide in 2003.

GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS & FAVORITES

Santa Anita Handicap G1 at Santa Anita Park, Saturday, March 8 : Shaping up to be one of the biggest races of the year as Mucho Macho Man and Will Take Charge have a rivalry to settle after the thrilling Breeders' Cup Classic finish. Plus, Game On Dude looms in the shadows as he looks to go for his third Big 'Cap victory.

Mucho Macho Man - is coming off a dominant 14 length win in the Florida Sunshine Millions Classic at Gulfstream January 18 after the biggest win of his life - an impressive score over Will Take Charge in the Breeders' Cup Classic and after looking terrific in his previous 11 starts overall as well - five wins, two second, three thirds and 1 unfortunate DNF at last year's Sunshine Million Classic at Gulfstream in January.

Will Take Charge - 2013 Eclipse Award winner for three year old colt was one of the only colts to race all three legs of the Triple Crown finishing eighth in the Kentucky Derby, seventh in the Preakness and tenth in the Belmont. This after an impressive win in the Grade 2 Rebel at Oaklawn Park in March. Read More

Tampa Bay Derby G2 at Tampa Bay Downs, Saturday, March 8 : Vinceremos - impressive Todd Pletcher trained colt is coming off a terrific win in the Sam F. Davis Stakes on this track February 1 in his third career start. He debuted with a second and broke maiden January 4 at Gulfstream before his stakes debut. Vinceremos will collect a $1 million bonus if he wins the Tampa Bay Derby and the Kentucky Derby in May. Edgar Prado gets the mount from post 6 on Vinceremos who is the 120 pound highweight for the Tampa Bay Derby. Read More

Gulfstream Park Handicap G2 at Gulfstream Park: Palace Malice - Todd Pletcher trained colt parlayed his enormous 3 1/4 length win in the Belmont Stakes June 8 with a win over Will Take Charge and Moreno by 9 1/2 lengths in the Grade 2 Jim Dandy July 27. This after a disappointing twelfth in the Kentucky Derby. He was fourth in the Grade 1 Travers in August but bounced back with a good second behind Ron the Greek in the Grade 1 Jockey Club Gold Cup at Belmont Park September 28. Read More

GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS & RACE FAVORITES

Shoemaker Mile Stakes 2013 at Hollywood Park (Grade 1): Jeranimo - Jeranimo returned to prominence last June with a win in this very race after a decent second in the race before - the Grade 3 San Francisco Mile at Golden Gate. He was third in the Grade 2 Del Mar Mile August 26 - the third straight race in which he placed. After that was a slight rough patch - a sixth in the Breeders' Cup Mile and a fourth in the Grade 2 Citation Handicap at Hollywood Park November 23. His first start of 2013 resulted in a nice bounce back for Jeranimo - a win in the Grade 2 San Gabriele Stakes at Santa Anita January 13 but he finished last of seven last time out in the Woodford Reserve Turf Classic at Churchill Downs May 4. Jeranimo looks to become the first repeat winner of this race since 2000 and will need a strong bounce back in order to achieve that goal. He starts from the rail Saturday. Read More

Triple Bend Handicap 2013 at Hollywood Park (Grade 1): Camp Victory - had an impressive win over rival The Factor in last year's Triple Bend Handicap but has struggled a tad since - a third in the Grade 2 Pat O'Brien August 26 and a sixth in the Grade 1 Santa Anita Sprint Championship October 6. He raced to a disappointing eleventh in the Breeders' Cup Turf in November and has just two starts since - a fifth in the Grade 2 Potrero Grande Stakes April 6 at Santa Anita and an OK third last time out in the Grade 3 Los Angeles Handicap here in May. Camp Victory won this race last year and was second in 2011 - needless to say he loves this stage! Read More

New York Stakes 2013 at Belmont Park (Grade 2): Dream Peace - Group 2 winner in France has raced all around the world with mixed results - four wins, two seconds and seven third place finishes in 16 career starts. She has raced to three consecutive third place finishes - last time out in the Grade 3 Beaugay Stakes at Belmont Park May 4, in the Grade 3 E.P. Taylor Stakes at Woodbine back in October (she won the E.P. Taylor in 2011) and in Grade 1 Flower Bowl Invitational in September. She also holds a very good second in the Grade 1 Diana Stakes at Saratoga July 28 and a pair of pedestrian results overseas. Dream Peace has placed in her last four starts overall and is the morning line favorite for the New York Stakes at 5-2. Read More

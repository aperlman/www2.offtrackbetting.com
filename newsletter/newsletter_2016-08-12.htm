G1 Fourstardave Handicap Preview, Entries & Odds at Saratoga

The 32nd running of the Fourstardave will be the first time as a Grade 1 race. The $500,000 race over the Saratoga Inner Turf course at a one mile distance drew a field of nine. Post time on Saturday for the Fourstardave is 6:32 p.m. as the 10th race on a 11-race card at Saratoga Race Course.

Grand Arch (8-1), Jim and Susan Hill's 7-year-old gelding will look to repeat his win in last year's race. He finished second in the 2014 edition to Seek Again. The race has drawn a quality field, including two horses from the red-hot Chad Brown barn in A Lot (12-1) and Takeover Target (7-2). The field includes the Oceanport winner (Blacktype, 10-1), the top two finishers in the Dixie (Takeover Target and Force the Pass, 8-1).

Del Mar Wraps Up Bing Crosby Meet With Action-Packed Weekend

Del Mar Racetrack will wrap up it's 3rd Bing Crosby season with an action packed weekend of races including the G1 Hollywood Derby, G2 Bayakoa Handicap & the G1 Matriarch Stakes.

G1 Hollywood Derby Preview, Entries & Odds

Camelot Kitten 7-2 Chad Brown trained colt has five wins and three runner-up finishes in 10 career starts. He finished a strong second in the G3 Dixiana Bourbon Stakes as a juvenile at Keeneland last October 4 and went on to a disappointing twelfth in the Breeders' Cup Juvenile Turf. Camelot Kitten has been terrific lately (three wins and a runner-up finish in his last four starts and hopes to keep the strong run going from gate 2 Saturday. Irad Ortiz has been tabbed to ride. G2 Bayakoa Handicap Preview, Entries & Odds

A field of eight older females have been confirmed for the Bayakoa Handicap - race 6 on Del Mar's Saturday card. Vale Dori 7-5 - Bob Baffert trained Argentinean bred filly has four wins, two seconds and a third place finish in nine starts all around the world including a win in a Group 1 race at home in May, 2015. G1 Matriarch Stakes Preview, Entries & Odds

A field of 12 of some of the best female turfers around will take to the track in the Matriarch Stakes - race 7 on Del Mar's Sunday card. Miss Temple City - has placed in 10 of 15 career starts for trainer Graham Motion and enters off an OK fifth in the Breeders' Cup Mile which followed a terrific win in the G1 Shadwell Turf Mile at Keeneland October 8.

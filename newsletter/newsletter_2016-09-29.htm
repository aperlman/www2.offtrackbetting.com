Santa Anita Park Offers Five Grade 1 Breeders' Cup Challenge 'Win & You're In' Stakes

Santa Anita Park's 23-day Autumn Meet, opens with a bang featuring five Grade I, Breeders' Cup Challenge "Win & You're In" prep stakes on Saturday, Sept. 30.

On opening day, fans will be treated to the Grade III, Eddie D. Stakes, which serves as a prep for the Grade I Breeders' Cup Turf Sprint on Nov. 5. The following day, Saturday, Oct. 1, a total of five Grade I Breeders' Cup Challenge "Win & You're In" stakes will be offered, highlighted by the Grade I Awesome Again Stakes, which is expected to attract superstar California Chrome 1-5 - the leading Thoroughbred in North America with earnings over $13 million continues to remain in first-place in this week's NTRA Top Thoroughbred Poll.

G1 Joe Hirsch Turf Classic Race Preview at Belmont Park - Flintshire 1-1 - Dominant in New York turf races in 2016, Juddmonte Farms homebred Flintshire will try for a fourth straight graded stakes win and third grade I score this season when he faces only three other horses in the 1 1/2-mile test on the Widener turf.

G1 Zenyatta Stakes Race Preview at Santa Anita Park - Three-time Eclipse champion Beholder 1-1 and last year's 3-year-old Filly Eclipse winner, Stellar Wind 9-5 will meet for the third time this Saturday at Santa Anita in the Grade I, $300,000 Zenyatta Stakes, final steppingstone to the $2 million Breeders' Cup Distaff and a likely engagement with the undefeated Songbird.

G1 Vosburgh Stakes Race Preview at Belmont Park - A. P. Indian 1-1 - Green Lantern Stables' A. P. Indian, a perfect five-for-five this season, will seek his second "Win and You're In" berth to the TwinSpires Breeders' Cup Sprint (gr. I) when he faces seven rivals in Belmont Park's Vosburgh Stakes (gr. I). Arnaud Delacour trained gelding has placed in 14 of 16 career starts - an impressive 10 wins and four runner-up finishes.

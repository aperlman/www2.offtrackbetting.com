Belmont Park offers $300,000 Guaranteed Late Pick 4 on Saturdays

With 21 graded stakes worth $5 million, Belmont Park's fall meet will showcase a stellar lineup when it opens for its 37-day meet on Saturday, September 10. Opening Saturday also marks the first $300,000 Guaranteed Late Pick 4, which will be offered on Saturdays throughout the 2011 fall meet. Read More

Turfway Park Opens This Thursday introducing a new 50-cent Pick 5 wager.

Live Thoroughbred racing returns to Turfway Park on September 8, with a packed slate of events over 16 racing days, a new Pick 5 wager, and the renaissance of the Kentucky Cup Day of Champions under sponsorship by WinStar Farm. Turfway introduces the daily 50-cent Pick 5 wager to its menu this season, replacing the Pick 6. The new wager requires players to choose the winners of five consecutive races beginning with the 3rd race. If no perfect tickets are sold, the wager pays 25 percent of the pool to those holding the most winners and the rest carries over to the next racing day. Bet Now

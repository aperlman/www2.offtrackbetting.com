The Road to the Kentucky Derby continues at Aqueduct with the G3 Gotham Stakes

Shagaf will put his unbeaten record on the line Saturday while stepping up to stakes competition on the Kentucky Derby trail.

Running for the same ownership as early Derby favorite Mohaymen, Shadwell Stable, the son of Bernardini drew post No. 2 in a field of eight for the Grade III, $400,000 Gotham Stakes at Aqueduct Racetrack in New York.

Santa Ysabel Stakes at Santa Anita Park - The Santa Ysabel Stakes will be run for the 49th time this year. To date, no winner of the Santa Ysabel has gone on to win the Kentucky Oaks. Songbird - Undefeated champion Songbird had an impressive four furlong workout Sunday under regular rider Mike Smith. The daughter of Medaglia d'Oro trained by Jerry Hollendorfer for Rick Porter's Fox Hill Farm was timed in 48 seconds flat, breezing.

Mac Diarmida Stakes at Gulfstream Park - Twilight Eclipse - is the world record holder at 1 1/2 miles on turf when he won the Grade 2 William McKnight Handicap December 28, 2013 in wire-to-wire fashion by seven lengths. He's placed in 10 of 12 races ahead of this weekend - a seventh five starts back in the Grade 3 Manhattan Stakes at Belmont Park June 7 and an eighth last time out in the Breeders' Cup Turf were the lone bad races over the last 18 months.

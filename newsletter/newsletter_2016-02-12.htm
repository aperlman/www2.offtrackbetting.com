The Road to the Kentucky Derby continues with the El Camino Real Derby

The 2016 edition of the Grade 3 El Camino Real Derby at Golden Gate Fields has attracted a full field of 14 runners looking to earn some Kentucky Derby points and the lion's share of the $200,000 purse. This should be a good betting race given the field size and the great mix of runners. There's a few international shippers, a few horses trying a synthetic track for the first time, and some that are going a route distance for the first time.

Barbara Fritchie Handicap Preview at Laurel Park - Hot City Girl and Lady Sabelia will be the top choices in the $300,000 Barbara Fritchie at Laurel Park on Saturday, and they appear to have the sharpest early speed. While the seven-furlong Fritchie isn't a match race, it certainly could be decided on the front end.

All eyes will be on Belmont Park in Elmont, New York this Saturday, June 11 when the third leg of the Triple Crown, the Grade 1 Belmont Stakes will be run. Exaggerator, 9-5 favorite, drew the No. 11 post out of 13 horses, and that position has led to three winners in the previous 14 years (2002, 2003 and 2014).

The top challengers for Exaggerator look to be Stradivari (No. 5 post position) and Suddenbreakingnews (No. 4), who are both listed at 7-1 betting odds.

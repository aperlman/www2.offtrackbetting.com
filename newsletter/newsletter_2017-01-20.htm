Awesome Slew Headlines Sunshine Millions Classic

Awesome Slew, a Florida-bred who has distinguished himself as a graded stakes winner, has yet to race in restricted company, but that will change Saturday when he heads a field of eight in the Sunshine Millions Classic at Gulfstream Park.

The 15th running of the Classic is one of five stakes for horses bred in the Sunshine State on the 12-race card. A 4-year-old homebred for Live Oak Plantation, Awesome Slew enters the Classic, an 1 1/8-mile race for runners 4 years old and up, following a third-place finish in the Harlan's Holiday (G3) at Gulfstream Park Dec. 17.

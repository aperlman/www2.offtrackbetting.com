UNDEFEATED MASTERY STRETCHES OUT IN GRADE I CASHCALL FUTURITY

Mastery 1-2, who is undefeated in two starts for Hall of Fame trainer Bob Baffert, will stretch out and step up in the $300,000-guaranteed Los Alamitos CashCall Futurity Saturday at Los Alamitos.

The race for 2-year-olds is part of a Grade I doubleheader on the third day of the Winter meet. Post time Saturday is 12:30 p.m. The Futurity is the sixth of nine races and has an approximate post time of 3 p.m. Won previously by stars such as Snow Chief, A.P. Indy, Best Pal, Real Quiet, Point Given, Lookin At Lucky, Shared Belief and Dortmund, the CashCall Futurity is part of the "Road to the Kentucky Derby" series. Saturday's winner will receive 10 points towards securing a spot in the starting gate May 6, 2017 at Churchill Downs.

G1 Starlet Stakes Preview, Entries & Odds at Los Alamitos

The $300,000-guaranteed Starlet for 2-year-old fillies & will be run at 1 1/16 miles. The Starlet, whose past winners include Althea, Very Subtle, Goodbye Halo, Sardula, Serena's Song, Surfside, Blind Luck and Take Charge Brandi, is part of the "Road to the Kentucky Oaks" series. The winner Saturday will receive 10 points towards earning a berth in the Kentucky Oaks next May 5 in Louisville.

American Gal 5-2 - up-and-coming Bob Baffert trained filly has three career starts and two wins and a third place finish. She made her graded stakes debut last time out and finished a very respectable third as favorite - after a troubled trip in the Breeders' Cup Juvenile Fillies November 5. American Gal has been competitive every time out and looks to be in good shape to claim her first graded stakes score. Regular rider Mike Smith will try to accomplish that goal from post 7.

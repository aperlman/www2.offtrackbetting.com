GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS & FAVORITES

FRIZETTE STAKES at Belmont Park (Grade 1): Dreaming of Julia - Todd Pletcher filly enters the Frizette having started two career races and winning both - a win on August 6 by an astounding ten and a half lengths at Saratoga was followed by an equally impressive 16 1/4 length score in the Meadow Star Classic last time out here on September 9.

My Happy Face - has two career starts as well - both Maiden Special Weight races. Last time out was a win at Saratoga on August 31 by an amazing 21 1/4 lengths to follow up a second to Teen Pauline in her debut on that same track July 25. Read More

2012 HAWTHORNE GOLD CUP HANDICAP at Hawthorne Race Course (Grade 2): The Grade 2 Hawthorne Gold Cup Handicap comes our way from Hawthorne Race Course in Cicero Illinois this Saturday, October 6. Alternation - comes into this race on quite a role - six starts in 2012 with five wins including the Grade 3 Pimlico Special Stakes, the Grade 2 Oaklawn Handicap on April 14, the Grade 2 Razorback Handicap on March 10 and the Essex Handicap on February 4 - he swept the three stakes at Oaklawn Park for older horses this year.

Cease - has six starts this year with two wins in the Maxxam Gold Cup Handicap and an Allowance Optional Claiming, a second in an Allowance Optional Claiming race, a very nice third last time out in the Grade 1 Woodward, a fourth in the Grade 3 Ben Ali Stakes at Keeneland on April 22 and a fifth in the Grade 3 Louisville Handicap on May 26. Cease's best work is done on dirt - just the surface that the Gold Cup happens to be run on.Read More

SHADWELL TURF MILE STAKES at Keeneland (Grade 1): The Grade 1 Shadwell Turf Mile Stakes is on tap for Keeneland in Lexington Kentucky this Saturday, October 6. Wise Dan - Wise Dan ended his 2011 campaign with impressive wins in the Grade 2 Fayette Stakes and the Grade 1 Clark Handicap and started 2012 with yet another win - the Grade 3 Ben Ali Stakes at Keeneland in April. He continued his strong 2012 campaign with a decent second in the Grade 1 Stephen Foster Handicap at Churchill Downs on June 16 and followed that with an impressive win by five lengths in the Grade 2 Fourstardave Handicap at Saratoga on August 11. Last time out was yet another win in the Grade 1 Woodbine Mile September 16. That makes six wins in his last seven starts for the favorite in this race - he is definitely the one everyone will have their eyed fixed upon. Read More

FOXWOODS CHAMPAGNE STAKES at Belmont Park (Grade 1): Archwarrior - is one of three Todd Pletcher colts entered in the Champagne and has a ton of hype behind him. He has only one start but it was an impressive one - a Maiden Special Weight score by 3 3/4 lengths at Saratoga August 30.

Shanghai Bobby - has three career stars and three wins for trainer Todd Pletcher. Last time out was an impressive 3 3/4 length score in the Grade 2 Three Chimneys Hopeful Stakes to follow a maiden win on April 19 at Aqueduct and an impressive win in the Track Barron Stakes at Belmont Park on June 27. Shanghai Bobby looks good and should be in contention Saturday.Read More

American Pharoah Racing in Travers Stakes Saturday at Saratoga

American Pharoah 1-5 - the 12th horse to ever win the Kentucky Derby, the Preakness and the Belmont Stakes - the first since Affirmed in 1978 will try to build on a sensational eight race win streak. 2-year-old Champion male of 2014 American Pharoah hits the track for the tenth time looking unstoppable.

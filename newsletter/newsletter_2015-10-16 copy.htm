LAUREL PARK - Nine Stakes Races for Jim McKay Maryland Million Day

Jim McKay Maryland Million Day is one of the premier afternoons in Maryland racing, with nine stakes races showcasing the most talented runners in the state. The star of the $1 million showcase for horses sired by accredited Maryland stallions is once again expected to be Ben's Cat, who is scheduled to make his sixth consecutive Maryland Million appearance.

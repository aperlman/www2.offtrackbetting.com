The Road to the Kentucky Derby continues with the G2 Tampa Bay Derby

Brody's Cause heads Tampa Bay Derby field - Brody's Cause will face nine rivals in the east coast's next Kentucky Derby prep. He should be cranked having posted eight workouts in the last two moths, including one Sunday at Tampa Bay Downs that measured a half mile in 48.40 seconds, sixth-fastest of 72.

Corey Lanerie has the mount on the 122 pound highweight in Brody's Cause's first start in nearly four months - from gate 8.

Big 'Cap - G1 Santa Anita Handicap Race Preview - Effinex 2-1 the Big Cap's morning line favorite. Eclipse Award finalist for Older Male had eight starts last year for trainer James Jerkins. He also had a very good runner-up finish behind some horse named America Pharoah in the Breeders' Cup Classic October 31 and a third in the Grade 1 Jockey Club Gold Cup October 3 with a DNF in the Grade 2 Brooklyn Invitational and a fourth in the Grade 1 Woodward Stakes sprinkled in there...

Frank E. Kilroe Mile Race Preview at Santa Anita Park - Bolo's neck victory in the Arcadia Stakes on Feb. 13 could be a precursor of bigger and better things, says Mike Smith, who rides the four-year-old Temple City colt in Saturday's Grade I Frank E. Kilroe Mile on turf. Bolo - has nine career starts with four wins and two third place finishes for trainer Carla Gaines and enters off a career best effort in the aforementioned Arcadia...

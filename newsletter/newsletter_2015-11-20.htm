Exaggerator Heads Field For 2015 Delta Downs Jackpot Stakes

The Grade 3 Delta Downs Jackpot comes our way this Saturday, November 21 from Delta Downs Racetrack in Vinton, Louisiana. Exaggerator (2-1) heads a field of 10 two year olds who will be featured when they take to Delta Downs dirt track and go 1 1/16 miles for a lucrative $1 million purse and 10 qualifying points on the Road to the Kentucky Derby.

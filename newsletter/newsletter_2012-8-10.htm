LA JOLLA HANDICAP at Del Mar (Grade 2): The La Jolla has attracted a small field of five to go in race 8 on Del Mar's Saturday card. My Best Brother - won the first division of the Oceanside most recently by two lengths to follow up two other wins - a Maiden Special Weight and an Allowance Optional Claiming at Hollywood Park. Read More

GARDENIA STAKES at Ellis Park (Grade 3): The Grade 3 Gardenia Stakes comes our way from Ellis Park this Saturday, August 11. Fillies and mare three years old and upward will go one mile on Ellis Park's main track in search of their share of the $100,000 purse. Joyful Victory has been on the radar a while - she was favored in last year's Kentucky Oaks and finished fourth and followed that up with a respectable second in the Grade 1 Mother Goose. Read More

MONMOUTH OAKS at Monmouth Park (Grade 3): Morrow Cove - very impressive Todd Pletcher filly has won three of her last four starts including the Serena's Song Stakes by four and a half lengths here on July 15 and an Allowance race at Delaware Park a staggering 10 and three quarter lengths before that. She will be after her first graded stakes win on Saturday and is certainly one horse that everyone will keep an eye on. Read More

FOURSTARDAVE HANDICAP at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 2): Data Link - finished a respectable third in the Grade 3 Canadian Turf Stakes at Gulfstream on March 3 in his graded stakes debut and went on to win the Makers 46 Mile in impressive fashion. Last time out Data Link won the Grade 2 Monmouth Stakes in record time to follow a fifth in the Turf Classic Stakes at Churchill Downs. He has six wins in 12 career races and based on his last outing he has to be on the Fourstardave radar. Read More

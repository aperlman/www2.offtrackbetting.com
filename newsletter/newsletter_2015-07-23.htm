SARATOGA OPENS FRIDAY JULY 24 - BET ON THE SPA ALL SUMMER LONG

Two of the biggest days in North American racing will highlight the historic 147th meet at Saratoga Race Course, with the Grade 1, $1.25 million Travers the centerpiece of a supercharged Travers Day and the Grade 1, $1.25 million Whitney Handicap anchoring five stakes on Saturday, August 8 as part of the Fasig-Tipton Festival of Racing.

The 40-day meet, which includes 69 stakes worth a record $18.7 million in purses, will run from Friday, July 24, through Labor Day, Monday, September 7. After opening weekend, racing will be conducted six days a week, Wednesdays through Mondays. LOG IN >>

2015 NORTH AMERICA CUP - MOHAWK RACETRACK

The Pepsi North America Cup is the centerpiece of Mohawk's fantastic Saturday card of racing that will also feature the $479,000 final of the Fan Hanover Stakes (which will showcase three-year-old pacing fillies), the $350,000 final of the Roses Are Red (pacing mares), the $272,000 final of the Armbro Flight (trotting mares), the $233,000 final of the Goodtimes Stakes (three-year-old trotting colts), the $100,000 Mohawk Gold Cup (invitational pacers) and the $75,000 North America Cup consolation.

Round 2 Qualifying for the Bluffs Run $60K Juvenile Stakes

Bluffs Run Greyhound Racing: The 2-year-old greyhounds who survived Round 1 of the Bluffs Run $60,000 Juvenile Stakes will be back in action on Saturday as the Iowa track hosts Round 2 qualifying races.

The Bluffs Run card starts at 5:00 p.m. ET / 2:00 p.m. PT, and it will have 12 big races to wager on. The five-sixteenths-of-a-mile Round 2 Qualifying Races are going as Races 1-8 on the card, followed by the five-sixteenths-of-a-mile, Grade TC Iowa Bred Feature in Race 9. The 48 fastest juvenile greyhounds who make the cut on Saturday will come back for the Round 3 quarterfinals on Wednesday, June 24th. The Round 4 semi-finals will be held on Saturday, June 27th, with the Bluffs Run $60,000 Juvenile Stakes Championship final race going on Independence Day, Saturday, July 4th. Log In

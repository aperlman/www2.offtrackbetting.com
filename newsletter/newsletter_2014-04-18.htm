GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS & FAVORITES

Lexington Stakes 2014 (G3) at Keeneland, Saturday, April 19 : The Grade 3 Coolmore Lexington Stakes is on tap for Keeneland Race Course this Saturday, April 19 - it offers one final chance for three year olds to sneak into the Kentucky Derby in a couple weeks time. Three year old Derby hopefuls will take to Keeneland's dirt track and race 1 1/16 miles looking for the winner's share of the $200,000 purse and the all-important 10 qualifying points toward the Kentucky Derby May 3.

Divine Oath - Todd Pletcher trained colt has just two career starts with two wins - an Allowance Optional Claiming and a MSW - both at Gulfstream. He sat out of the Blue Grass Stakes in favor of making his stakes debut this weekend and is expected to be the one to beat. Javier Castellano gets the call from post 6 Saturday. Read More

Mr Speaker - Shug McGaughey trained colt has looked terrific at the end of 2013 - an impressive 10 3/4 length win in the Grade 3 Dania Beach Stakes here on December 21 and a MSW score at Belmont Park in October. Last time out was a good second in the Grade 3 Palm Beach Stakes at Gulfstream March 1 after a step backward - a disappointing seventh in the Grade 2 Holy Bull Stakes here January 25 in his first start on dirt. Mr Speaker has two wins and a second in his last four starts overall and is one to keep an eye on this weekend in his Polytrack debut. He leaves from the rail with Jose Lezcano aboard. Read More

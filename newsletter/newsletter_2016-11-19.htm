Gunnevera tops field of 10 for $1 Million Delta Downs Jackpot

Horses who last raced in California, Kentucky, and New York are set to descend on the rural community of Vinton, La., next Saturday for the Grade 3, $1 million Delta Downs Jackpot. On Friday, 10 were entered for the race, led by Grade 2 Saratoga Special winner Gunnevera.

The Jackpot will anchor a card of eight stakes worth $2.3 million. The program also features the Grade 3, $400,000 Delta Downs Princess, which also drew 10 entries on Friday. Delta plans to offer an all-stakes pick four with a guaranteed pool of $200,000. It will run on races 4-7 and have a reduced takeout of 12 percent. First post will be 1:45 p.m. Central.

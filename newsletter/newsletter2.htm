Off Track Betting Highlights

Welcome to OffTrackBetting.com! Off Track Betting is an Advance Deposit Wagering (ADW) service providing legal and secure phone and online horse betting on Thoroughbred, Harness and Quarter horse racing. Licensed, based and regulated in the U.S.A., Off Track Betting offers online horse racing betting from Lien Games Racing LLC, a licensed Service Provider by the North Dakota Racing Commission.

Click here to view our 2011 Off Track Betting license issued by the North Dakota Gaming Commission.

All horse racing wagers placed through Off Track Betting are commingled with partnered host track pari-mutuel pools, and winning wagers are paid at full track odds. Free membership allows horseplayers to bet horse racing online or by telephone, and also entitles all Off Track Betting members to real time audio, video feeds and experienced customer service. OTB offers horse racing betting on over 100 race tracks.

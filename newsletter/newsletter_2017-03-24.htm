Sunland Derby winner to get 50 Points towards the KY Derby

Sunday's $800,000 Sunland Derby (G3) offers points on a 50-20-10-5 basis toward a Kentucky Derby berth and Conquest Mo Money is the lone stakes winner among 12 betting interests. The unbeaten colt exits convincing wins in the Mine That Bird Derby and Riley Allison at Sunland, but he will face a class check with 10 rivals invading from across the country for the 1 1/8-mile race.

Kitten's Cat leads field for Turfway's G3 Spiral Stakes

Kitten's Cat is the 4-1 morning-line favorite for Saturday's $500,000 Grade II JACK Cincinnati Casino Spiral Stakes at Turfway Park in Florence, Kentucky. The Spiral is a Kentucky Derby prep race that will offer points on a 50-20-10-5 scale to the top four finishers.

Dubai World Cup: Favorite Arrogate primed for $10 million race

Arrogate is one race away from becoming the highest earner the sport has seen. The $6m first prize in the Dubai World Cup here on Saturday, for which he will set off as the long odds-on favorite, would take Arrogate's career earnings past $17m, around three-quarters of which would have been banked in the space of the past two months.

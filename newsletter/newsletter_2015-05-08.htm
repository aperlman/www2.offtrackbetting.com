MONMOUTH PARK OPENS SATURDAY

Monmouth Park opens this Saturday, May 9, with a 12-race program attracting a total of 126 entries. Topping the day's card is the $75,000 Decathlon Stakes for 3-year-olds and upward, which features a field of 14, including graded stakes winners Prudhoe Bay, victor of last year's Jersey Shore, and Favorite Tale, winner of the Gallant Bob at Parx.

A six-furlong dash, the Decathlon is the first of this year's stakes events, which includes 17 graded stakes. As always, the centerpiece of Monmouth's summer will be the $1 million William Hill Haskell Invitational, which has its 48th running on Sunday, August 2. Monmouth's Grade 1 turf race, the $500,000 United Nations Stakes, will be the highlight of the Fourth of July weekend when it's renewed on Sunday, July 5.

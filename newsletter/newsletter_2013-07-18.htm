GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS, ENTRIES & ML ODDS

Schuylerville Stakes at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 3): BREEDERS' CUP JUVENILE FILLIES: Saratoga Race Course in Saratoga Springs, New York will be the scene of the Grade 3 Schuylerville Stakes on opening Friday, July 19. Two year old fillies will be featured as they sprint six furlongs on Saratoga's dirt track in search of the winner's share of the $150,000 purse. Read More

James Marvin Stakes at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 3): BREEDERS' CUP SPRINT : The James Marvin Stakes has attracted a field of nine for race 8 on Saratoga's Opening Friday card. Post time is 4:47 PM. Here is a brief glance into the past performances of all nine entries for this Grade 3 stakes - a local prep for the Grade 1 Forego Stakes August 31. Read More

Coaching Club American Oaks at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 1): BREEDERS' CUP LADIES' CLASSIC: Princess Of Sylmar - Todd Pletcher pupil has won five of her last six starts - a second in the Grade 2 Gazelle Stakes to Close Hatches at Aqueduct April 6 being the only blemish in the last nine months. The wins include the Grade 1 Kentucky Oaks last time out at 38-1 odds over Dreaming of Julia, the minor Busher Stakes at Aqueduct February 2 to follow another win at Aqueduct - the Busanda Stakes by 7 1/2 lengths January 4. She also has a MSW and an Allowance Optional Claiming score. She has emerged on the radar and is the name to watch Saturday. Read More

Ontario Matron Stakes at Woodbine Racetrack (Grade 3): BREEDERS' CUP LADIES' CLASSIC. Sisterly Love - has seven career starts for trainer Mark Casse - two Allowance scores at Gulfstream and a MSW win at Calder October 27 were followed by an OK third in her stakes debut - the Houston Ladies Classic Stakes behind Joyful Victory January 26. She went on to a fifth in the Grade 2 Top Flight Handicap in March and a disappointing eighth as 2-1 favorite in the Grade 3 Whimsical Stakes on this track in April but bounced back last time out with a nice 8 1/2 length win in the minor Trillium Stakes on this track June 8. She won her first stakes races last time out and looks good to record her first graded stakes score Saturday. Read More

Delaware Handicap 2013 at Delaware Park (Grade 1): BREEDERS' CUP LADIES' CLASSIC.: Royal Delta - back-to-back Female Horse of the Year in the United States for trainer Bill Mott has looked exceptional of late except for a doomed trip to Dubai where she finished tenth in the World Cup. Before the trip she had three straight wins including the Grade 3 Sabin Stakes at Gulfstream, a repeat win in the Breeders' Cup Ladies' Classic and a win in the Grade 1 Beldame at Belmont Park September 29. Last time out she was a decent second in the Grade 2 Fleur de Lis Handicap at Churchill Downs. She won this race last year over Tiz Miz Sue who has opted to skip the Del Cap and has three wins and a pair of seconds in six starts since. She is the highweight and the favorite for Saturday's race and she will have the extra motivation of rebounding from an ugly and uncharacteristic fifth last time out. Read More

Shuvee Handicap at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 3): BREEDERS' CUP LADIES' CLASSIC: The Grade 3 Shuvee Handicap goes down at Saratoga Race Course this Saturday, July 20. Fillies and mares three years old and upward will take to Saratoga's dirt track and go 1 1/8 miles for a purse of $200,000. A strong field of seven females will line up in the Shuvee Handicap - race 9 on Saratoga's Saturday schedule. Post time is 5:12 PM. Here is a brief glance into the past performances of all seven combatants for this Grade 3 stakes - two of which are cross entered in the Grade 1 Delaware Handicap that same day. Read More

Spring Meets open at Keeneland, Woodbine, Emerald Downs & Lone Star Park

It is going to be an action-packed weekend at OffTrackBetting.com! Keeneland Racecourse opens its spring meeting today and the Derby trail comes marching through tomorrow afternoon in the $1 million Grade 1 Toyota Blue Grass Stakes. In all, there are 7 Grade 1 races available between Keeneland, Santa Anita & Aqueduct through Sunday.

In addition to Keeneland, other tracks opening include Woodbine, Emerald Downs & Lone Star Park. If you have not logged in for awhile, now might be the time as the Kentucky Derby prep races have been fantastic!

G1 Blue Grass Stakes Preview, Entries & Odds - The Grade 1 Toyota Blue Grass Stakes comes our way this Saturday, April 9 from Keeneland in Lexington, Kentucky. Zulu 5-2 Two Trained by Todd Pletcher - morning line favorite Zulu, second in the Fountain of Youth, as well as impressive allowance winner Donegal Moon - are among the other headliners in an overflow field of 3-year-olds drawn Tuesday morning.

G1 Santa Anita Derby Preview, Entries & Odds - Mor Spirit 8-5 Three year olds will be featured when they take to Santa Anita's dirt track and race 1 1/8 miles. Bob Baffert trained Ringling has six career starts with three wins and three second place finishes - favored in every race so far. Last time out was a second place finish behind Danzing Candy in the Grade 2 San Felipe Stakes on this track March 12 after terrific win in the Grade 3 Robert B. Lewis Stakes...

G1 Wood Memorial Preview, Entries & Odds - Eight three year olds will line up in the Wood Memorial - race 10 on Aqueduct's stacked Saturday schedule. Shagaf 2-1 has three career starts and three wins. He stepped into stakes company for the first time last time out and won the Grade 3 Gotham Stakes by 1 1/4 lengths on this track March 5.

G1 Carter Handicap Preview, Entries & Odds - Aqueduct Racetrack in Queens, New York will play host to the Grade 1 Carter Handicap on Saturday, April 9. Salutos Amigos 2-1 has won six of his last 12 starts (all stakes) for David Jacobson & before his latest win Salutos Amigos had consecutive runner-up finishes...

In addition, Keeneland on Saturday will also be featuring the Grade 1 Madison Stakes & Ashland Stakes , & Woodbine will host the Grade 3 Whimsical Stakes on Sunday.

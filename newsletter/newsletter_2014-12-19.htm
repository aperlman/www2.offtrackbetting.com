LOS ALAMITOS FUTURITY PREVIEW - GRADE 1

Los Alamitos Futurity - (Grade 1) : The Grade 1 Los Alamitos Futurity, formerly the Hollywood CashCall Futurity comes our way from Los Alamitos Race Course in California this Saturday, December 20 - a race that was moved to this track after the closing of Hollywood Park. Some of the best two year olds in the land will be after the winner's share of the $500,000 purse and the 10 qualifying points toward next year's Kentucky Derby when they go 1 1/16 miles on Los Alamitos' dirt track.

Dortmund - early favorite for next year's Kentucky Derby will try to remain perfect in his career for trainer Bob Baffert - two career starts so far and two wins. He makes his stakes debut Saturday off an Allowance Optional Claiming score by 7 3/4 lengths on Stars of Tomorrow 2 Day at Churchill Downs November 29 and a 4 3/4 length maiden score in his debut - at Santa Anita November 2. He leaves from post 3 Saturday with Martin Garcia in the saddle in Dortmund's first try around two turns.

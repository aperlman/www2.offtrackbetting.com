FLOWER BOWL INVITATIONAL at Belmont Park (Grade 1): $1 million will be on the line for fillies and mares three years old and upward as they go 1 1/4 miles on Belmont's inner turf track. The Flower Bowl is yet another Breeders' Cup Win and You're In event - this one for the Filly and Mares Turf Classic. Zagora - Zagora appears to be the class of this race after great results recently and a good career in Europe. She has won four of her last six races - the Grade 3 Endeavor, the Grade 3 Hillsborough and the Grade 3 Gallorette and finished third in the Grade 1 Diana ahead of Hunger Island on July 28.

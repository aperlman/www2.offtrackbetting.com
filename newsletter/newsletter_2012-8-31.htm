GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS & FAVORITES

WOODWARD STAKES at Saratoga (Grade 1): Saratoga in upstate New York will be the scene of the Grade 1 Woodward Stakes this Saturday, September 1. Mucho Macho Man - has been very impressive in 2012 but is still without a Grade 1 win. A win at the Florida Sunshine Millions Stakes over Ron the Greek and Turbo Compressor January 28 preceded a very good win in the Grade 2 Gulfstream Handicap on March 10.Read More

FOREGO STAKES at Saratoga (Grade 1): A field of eight three year olds will line up in race 9 on Saratoga's Saturday card with a post time of 5:12 PM. Shackleford won the 2011 Preakness, finished fourth in the Derby and fifth in the Belmont Stakes - he has to be considered a serious contender in this one. He won the Grade 2 Churchill Downs Stakes on May 5 over Amazombie and followed that up with an equally impressive score in the Grade 1 Metropolitan Handicap over Caleb's Posse on May 28.Read More

DEBUTANTE STAKES at Del Mar (Grade 1): The Debutante has attracted a field of nine female juveniles for race 9 on Del Mar's Saturday card. Post time is 6:00 PM. Executiveprivilege - Bob Baffert trained filly is undefeated in three career starts and is the overwhelming favorite for the Debutante. Executiveprivilege broke maiden with a four and a half length win in a five and a half furlong Maiden Special Weight race at Hollywood Park on June 28 and followed that result with an impressive three and a quarter length win in the Landaluce Stakes.Read More

SEAWAY STAKES at Woodbine (Grade 3): The Grade 3 Seaway Stakes comes our way this Saturday, September 1 from beautiful Woodbine in Toronto Ontario. Roxy Gap - has three wins on this track this summer - the Grade 3 Royal North Stakes last time out, the Grade 2 Hendrie Stakes by two and a half lengths and the Grade 3 Whimsical Stakes on April 22. Sandwiched in between the wins is a pair of seconds - in the Grade 2 Nassau on grass to Marketing Mix and at the Sweet Briar Too Stakes on July 7.Read More

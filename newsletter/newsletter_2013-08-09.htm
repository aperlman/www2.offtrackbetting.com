GRADED STAKES RACE PREVIEWS, ENTRIES & ML ODDS

La Jolla Handicap at Del Mar Race Track (Grade 2): Greeley Awesome goes up against Freakin Rocket - A field of nine three year olds will take to the track in the La Jolla Handicap - race 8 on Del Mar's Saturday schedule. Post time is 5:40 PM. Let's take a brief look into the past performances of all nine combatants for this Grade 2 stakes - the last major prep for the Grade 2 Del Mar Derby September 1. Read More

Fourstardave Handicap at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 2): Wise Dan - How good was Wise Dan in 2012? He is the only horse since 1981 to win three Eclipse Awards in one night. He was the runaway winner in Horse of the Year, Four-year-old & up male and Male turf (3yo&up). He has won seven straight races ahead of the Fourstardave and 12 of his last 14 races overall including the Breeders' Cup in November. This year he looks to be on an Eclipse Award path once again - three starts and three impressive wins. Last time out was the Grade 3 Firecracker Handicap after the Woodford Reserve Turf Classic and the Maker's 46 Mile Stakes. He is simply the most dominant horse on the planet right now, is the defending champion in this race, will carry the highweight of 129 pounds and the one to beat Saturday! He is the 3-5 favorite on the morning line and will have John Velazquez in the saddle. Read More

Monmouth Oaks 2013 at Monmouth Park (Grade 3): Seaneen Girl - won the Grade 2 Golden Rod Stakesback in November after a second in the Grade 3 Mazarine Stakes at Woodbine in October. She had an OK third in the Grade 2 Fair Grounds Oaks March 30 after a four month layoff and followed that with an ugly ninth in the Grade 1 Kentucky Oaks. She has raced three straight Grade 3 stakes - a seventh in the Regret Stakes at Churchill Downs June 15, a good second in the Iowa Oaks June 29 and last time out a sixth in the Arlington Oaks. She has been inconsistent in her career but is the only stakes winner in the field. Saturday looks like a good opportunity to break a slump that dates back to November 24. Read More

Adirondack Stakes at Saratoga Race Course (Grade 2): Fiftyshadesofgold - began her career at Lone Star and posted a MSW victory by an impressive 10 lengths in that race. The Bret Calhoun trained filly next stepped up into stakes company and won the minor Debutante Stakes by an equally impressive eight lengths at Churchill Downs June 22. She is the co-highweight at 122 pounds, is the 7-5 favorite in her graded stakes debut and will have Corey Lanerie in the saddle. Read More

John C. Mabee Stakes at Del Mar Race Track (Grade 2): Tiz Flirtatious - has been terrific in her career with six wins, three seconds and a third place finish in 10 career starts. She is coming off a second in the Grade 1 Gamely Stakes behind multiple Grade 1 winner Marketing Mix after leading at Hollywood Park May 27 to follow a win in the Grade 2 Santa Ana Stakes over Lady of Shamrock, a win in the minor Valentine Dancer Stakes January 23 and a pair of seconds in graded stakes races - the Grade 3 Robert J. Frankel in December and the Grade 1 Matriarch Stakes in November. Tiz Flirtatious has been at or near the top in every career start, has never been worse than second in six starts on grass, is the 123 pound highweight and is the one to beat Sunday. She makes her first start at Del Mar however. Read More
